Sand Comparison for Corydoras Catfish

A view of the different types of sand available in the US that are deemed to be safe for corydoras catfish.

CaribSea Super Naturals

This sand is extremely fine. It's practically a powder and when on the hand, it 'sticks'. There are no sharp edges and it does not feel grainy. It's an off-white color.

QuikCrete Play Sand

Also a very soft sand, very fine, sort of powdery. This one is very close in feel to the CaribSea, but is not as 'sticky' on the hand.

QuikCrete Pool Filter Sand

A very natural looking sand with larger, varied grain size. Not sticky, nor sharp.
